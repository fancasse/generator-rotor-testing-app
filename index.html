AC Impedance Test

Apply AC voltage in 10 V steps (up to 120 V max) across the field winding. Record current at each step. Z = V / I. A step change indicates shorted turns.

RSO Test (Recurrent Surge Oscillography)

Inject high-frequency pulses into one end of the rotor and measure the reflected waveform at the other end. Compare Channel 1 and Channel 2 traces. CH1 − CH2 = 0 for a healthy rotor.
